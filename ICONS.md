## Notes

- **Total**: 200 items across 14 categories
- Items are intentionally generic (e.g. "Cheese" not "Cheddar") to maximize cross-cultural fit while remaining visually distinct as icons
- Some items that look similar as icons are listed separately because they are shopped for separately (e.g. Lemon / Lime, Butter / Cream Cheese) — icon style should make them clearly distinguishable
- Items like Olive Oil, Vegetable Oil, Vinegar share a similar bottle silhouette — icon design should use distinctive label colours or shapes to differentiate them
- "Oat Milk" is listed under Drinks rather than Dairy intentionally, as it is shelf-stable and shopped for differently
