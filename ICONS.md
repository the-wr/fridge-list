## Notes

- Items are intentionally generic (e.g. "Cheese" not "Cheddar") to maximize cross-cultural fit while remaining visually distinct as icons
- Some items share a similar silhouette — icon design should use distinctive colours or shapes to differentiate them (e.g. oil bottles, spice jars, shampoo vs conditioner)
- "Oat Milk" and "Almond Milk" are listed under Drinks rather than Dairy — they are shelf-stable and shopped for differently
- Baby & Pet is the most skippable category for users without children or pets; this is reflected in the default grid (TBD)
